About Holy Trinity International Mission

Christian Ministry in Nairobi, Kenya

Holy Trinity International Mission is a growing Christian ministry based in Nairobi, Kenya. We are committed to spreading the Gospel, empowering communities, and raising strong spiritual leaders. Our core goals include glorifying God through evangelism, training local leaders, supporting missionaries, and addressing both spiritual and physical needs in local and global communities.
