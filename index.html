Our Vision and Ministries

Mobilizing Churches in Mission

Mobilizing churches in mission means engaging every church to actively participate in God’s work through awareness, prayer, giving, and outreach. This is achieved by teaching biblical missions, forming mission teams, encouraging prayer and support, and creating partnerships and outreach programs. The goal is to see churches fully involved in praying, supporting, and sending missionaries.

Raising Leaders

Raising leaders focuses on developing spiritually mature, skilled, and servant-hearted individuals for church and mission work. This is done through leadership training, discipleship, mentoring, and workshops, while nurturing character and empowering emerging leaders—especially youth. The goal is to equip faithful leaders to effectively serve and lead in ministry.

Training and Sending Missionaries

Training and sending missionaries involves preparing individuals spiritually, culturally, and practically for effective ministry. This includes strong biblical teaching, cross-cultural understanding, evangelism skills, and ongoing support. The goal is to equip and send missionaries to share the Gospel and build sustainable Christian communities, while providing continuous care and guidance.

Partnership and Involvements

We mobilize churches and individuals to accelerate global missions through prayer, funding, training, and advocacy. By fostering united collaboration, this initiative strengthens, sustains, and expands missionary work to achieve the Great Commission. Key impacts include empowered leaders, effective missionary deployment, and broadened global outreach.
